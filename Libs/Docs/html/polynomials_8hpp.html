Horner method for polynomial evaluation.

Evaluates the polynomial and its derivative using Horner method.

Parameters
[in]xThe point at which to evaluate the polynomial.
[in]aArray of the coefficients. NOTE: a[0] is the constant term and a[degree] is the coefficient of x^n.
[in]degreeThe degree of the polynomial.
[out]dpdxThe value of the derivative.
Returns
The value of the polynomial.
